## Privacy

It does **not** read, collect, infer, transmit, or process real neural data. Runtime posture: a single `index.html`; no backend; no analytics; no trackers; no external scripts; no cookies. `localStorage` is used **only** for scores, settings, badges, streaks, and local progression вЂ” all on your device.

## Non-claim

This game is **not** the AxonOS kernel, **not** medical software, and **not** a clinical BCI system. It simulates a neural signal and teaches AxonOS concepts. It makes no claim to read a brain, process real EEG, or prove kernel guarantees.
